About The Site

Welcome to our Snow Forecasting App! This app helps users check their chances of snowfall up to 5 days in advance across the United States. Additionally, students and teachers can estimate the likelihood of a snow day based on their location and the current forecast.

Data Collection

Our forecast data is collected from the Open-Meteo Weather API, which provides live hourly forecasts using the GFS (Global Forecasting System) weather model ensembles. Location data comes from the Nominatim/OpenStreetMap public API, which offers precise coordinates for cities across the United States.

How it works

Simply enter your city and state! Using live weather data and the location of the forecast, our algorithm determines your chance of snow and school cancellations. While we can't guarantee official decisions from your school district, we provide an estimate based on live weather data to keep you informed.

Who We Are

We are students at the University of Maryland who love snow! We want to share our appreciation for this wonderful weather phemonomenon, and create an app that we would have loved to use.
